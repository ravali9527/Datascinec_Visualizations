
Dataset used : Used the data set from time series analysis Which has number of orders as feature based on Ware house,Product Code…

Feature engineering:

1)From the given data set extracted information about how many orders are given per year
2)How many orders are from a particular warehouse
3)How many orders are for a particular Product

From the extracted information we can deduce the product with highest orders,Most popular Ware house,Which year has the highest number of sales

2)Jypyter notebook(cufflinks & Plotly)
3)Matplot lib

Interactive Vizualizations:
